ABOUT

Hello!

I'm Jonny, a seeker of strong visual aesthetics who specializes in concept-driven, detail-oriented art and web development. I also enjoy exploring innovation and finding inspiration in anything I can get my hands and eyes on.

Whether I am communicating with the viewer through visual art or the user through web development, my goal is to always create a captivating experience. Along with my love for exploring new web technology, I strive to find creative solutions to problems to create innovative products that users will enjoy for years to come.
